CFP Calculator

CFP Calculator is an online website that computes a person’s Ideal Bodyweight (IBW) based on their height, weight and gender. It uses a series of formulas specifically for male and female namely:

1. Robinson Formula
2. Miller Formula
3. Hamwi Formula
4. Devine Formula

Each formula has its own equations to compute for a person’s Ideal Bodyweight (IBW)
